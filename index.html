about

I am a Doctoral Student in Machine Learning and a Fellow at the ETH AI Center, advised by Fanny Yang.

My research focuses on alignment and safety for language models, privacy-preserving machine learning, and causal inference. During my PhD, I have collaborated with Apple and Google DeepMind. Before ETH, I was a Research Scientist at Featurespace and conducted research at the University of Cambridge on conformal prediction with Adrian Weller MBE and on interpretability methods for causal inference with Mihaela van der Schaar.
