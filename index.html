about

I'm a PhD student in machine learning and a Fellow at the ETH AI Center, advised by Fanny Yang.

My research focuses on language model alignment and safety, privacy-preserving machine learning, and trustworthy machine learning more broadly. During my PhD, I spent time at Apple working on pre-training visual generation models, and at Google DeepMind working on language model post-training and distillation. Before that, I was a Research Scientist at Featurespace. I also spent time at the University of Cambridge, where I worked on conformal prediction with Adrian Weller MBE and on interpretability methods for causal inference with Mihaela van der Schaar.
